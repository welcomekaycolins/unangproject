About Me

Hi, I’m Colins. My journey started at sea, working as a seaman for several contracts before life steered me toward
a new path in the call center industry. It’s been a big shift, and I’m still figuring out where I truly belong,
but every experience has taught me something valuable about resilience and adaptability.

One thing I know for sure is that being a father to my 3-year-old daughter is the most fulfilling role I’ve ever had.
My greatest goal is to give her the best life possible, grounded in faith and love for God. She inspires me every day
to keep pushing forward, even when the road feels uncertain.

Outside of work, I enjoy woodworking—a craft that lets me create something tangible and meaningful. Recently,
I’ve started studying web design, and this website marks my very first project. It’s a small step toward exploring
new opportunities and building a future that combines creativity, technology, and purpose.
